Output is produced via DBMS_OUTPUT.PUT_LINE; SET SERVEROUTPUT ON must be active in the client to see it.

TC01 Duplicate Booking Prevention Pass

Business Rule: BR1 / TV1 · File: Tests/TC01_DuplicateBooking.sql
Goal
Confirm that sp_BookAppointment rejects a second booking for the same (patient_id, schedule_id) when the first is already Scheduled.
Preconditions
Seed data: patient 21 has a Scheduled appointment at schedule slot 6 (appointment_id = 21).
Steps
Call sp_BookAppointment(21, 6, 'In-Person', 'TC01 duplicate test', ...) a second time.
Expected
Procedure raises ORA-20004; the error message contains “scheduled appointment already exists”.
Pass criteria
Exception caught, message matches, no new row inserted.
=== TC01: Duplicate Booking Prevention ===

SETUP: Confirmed existing Scheduled appointment for patient 21 at schedule slot 6.
Caught expected error: ORA-20004: A scheduled appointment already exists for patient 21 at schedule slot 6.
PASS: Duplicate booking was correctly rejected.
TC01 RESULT: PASS

=== TC01 Complete ===

TC02 Cancel Before 24-Hour Window Pass

Business Rule: BR2 · File: Tests/TC02_CancelBefore24hr.sql
Goal
Confirm that sp_CancelAppointment rejects a cancellation attempt when the slot is less than 24 hours away.
Setup
Creates a fresh DoctorSchedule row for today, 30 minutes in the future, and books an appointment on it for patient 61.
Steps
Call sp_CancelAppointment on the new appointment.
Expected
Procedure raises ORA-20002 with “must be made at least 24 hours”. Appointment remains Scheduled.
Cleanup
Test deletes the appointment and schedule row it created.
=== TC02: Cancel Before 24-Hour Window ===

SETUP: Created schedule slot (ID: 209) for today at 16:19
SETUP: Created appointment (ID: 55) for patient 61
Caught expected error: ORA-20002: Cannot cancel appointment 55. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Current lead time: 0 hours.
PASS: Cancellation within 24-hour window was correctly rejected. Status remains Scheduled.
TC02 RESULT: PASS

CLEANUP: Removed test appointment and schedule slot.
=== TC02 Complete ===
Known quirk. If the test is run so close to midnight that SYSTIMESTAMP + 30 min rolls into tomorrow, the lead time computation can report a value ≥ 24 and the test will fail. The underlying procedure is still correct; the harness, not the rule, is sensitive to clock edge cases.

TC05 Reschedule History Audit Trail Pass

Business Rule: BR3 · File: Tests/TC05_RescheduleHistory.sql
Goal
Confirm that sp_RescheduleAppointment moves the appointment to a new slot and writes a matching AppointmentHistory row.
Preconditions
Seed appointment 22 is Scheduled on slot 18. Slot 19 is is_available = 1.
Steps
Record history row count, call sp_RescheduleAppointment(22, 19, 'TC05 reschedule test'), reread history.
Expected
History row count increases by one. Newest row has previous_schedule_id = 18, previous_status = 'Scheduled', new_status = 'Scheduled'. Appointment.schedule_id on row 22 is now 19.
Cleanup
Test reverts the appointment to slot 18 and deletes the test-specific history row. 
=== TC05: Reschedule History Audit Trail ===

SETUP: Appointment 22 confirmed. History rows before: 0
ACTION: sp_RescheduleAppointment executed successfully.
VERIFY: History rows after: 1
VERIFY: Appointment.schedule_id now: 19
VERIFY: Most recent history record values:
  previous_schedule_id = 18
  previous_status      = Scheduled
  new_status           = Scheduled
PASS: History record captures previous slot/status and appointment now points to new slot.
TC05 RESULT: PASS

CLEANUP: Reverted appointment 22 to original schedule slot and removed test history.
=== TC05 Complete ===
